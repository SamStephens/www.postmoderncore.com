Lots of new releases from Tangent Precipitate over the last few months ranging over widely varying sound palettes, and an overdue excursion from Starcrusher brings drone, randomness, and power to fuck with your head, on "Afternoon and Evening". And the just uploaded Stolen Art Gallery is an exhibition of peoples digital photos filched off their computers using person to person file sharing.
