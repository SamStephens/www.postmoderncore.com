Hollow Earth Radio have started broadcasting "A sequential discography of the Postmoderncore catalogue. From 001 - 070", playing one Postmoderncore album each week. The show broadcasts each Wednesday from 4:00am to 5:30am Pacific Time. Next week the second album in the Postmoderncore catalog will play, The Martian Canals.
