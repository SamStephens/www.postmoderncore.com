Tangent will play at the venue C.U.E with two Japanese performers, 石上和也 (Kazuya Ishigami) and 稲見 淳 (Sunao Inami). If you happen to be in Japan, check out the map, or give us an email to find out where to go. If you're not, the gig may be on live streaming video at http://www.cavestudio.org/cue/live_from_far_east/broadcast/. Or the gig will possibly be released on postmoderncore.
