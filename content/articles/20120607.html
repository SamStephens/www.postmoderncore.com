The Unknown Rockstar has had a track accepted to appear on the noise compilation SIGNALVOID, as well as a photo. The explanation from the SIGNALVOID website:

SIGNALVOID is a noise music compilation.

Participants create up to three noise tracks, each of exactly a minute in length, with no silence or fades at either the beginning or end of each piece.

The whole release will be designed to be played on shuffle. Submissions must be in 16-bit wav format and received by July 1st 2012. Tracks must be new/original material.
