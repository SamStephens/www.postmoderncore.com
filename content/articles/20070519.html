--- kind: article reported: 2007-05-19 title: Release of "Deep Earplug Music" and "Pickel" by The Unknown Rockstar thumbnail: unknown-rockstar-pickel.jpg thumbnail_url: "/albums/pickel" thumbnail_title: Pickel ---

The Unknown Rockstar has released two new albums, Deep Earplug Music and Pickel.

Deep Earplug Music was recorded live at the first Deep Earplug Music in 2006, and is a guitar based drone and tone piece.

Pickel is a pair of contrasting live echo guitar pieces.
