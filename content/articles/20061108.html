--- kind: article reported: 2005-11-08 title: Live Bones available for download - the live album of the Salad Bones gig thumbnail: saladbones.jpg thumbnail_url: http://www.saladbones.co.nz/livebones.html thumbnail_title: Salad bones ---

Dr Shopper has released the Live Bones album to the internet. It's edited from the recording of the Salad Bones CD release gig, and features Felix Preval, Sstimuluss, Jonny Potts, The Unknown Rockstar, Robyn Kenealy, Dick Whyte, C.W. Ireson, Lucola and T-Bone Vocelli.
