Welcome to Fredstock 2010, four days of brain fertilising music, sound, art and performances brought to you by the Frederick Street Sound and Light Exploration Society.

The Unknown Rockstar plays on Friday night, the 29th of October. The gig will start shortly after 7pm. Check out the Fredstock website to see all the other great acts that are playing.
