Happy. 9 p.m. $12, or $10 with costume.

When there is no more room in hell the dead will stomp the dance floor.

Postmoderncore presents the inaugaral Zombie Prom at Happy (cnr Tory and Vivian St). On Saturday April 22nd dig up your favourite funeral frock and live it up with the undead. Free Zombie compilation CD for the first 50 corpses to stuble through the door featuring music by Zombie Prom Queen, Starcruzer, Zombie?Fuck!, The Lovehaters, and The Mysterious Tape Man.

Monstrous music all night with (un)live acts:

Zombie Prom Queen, undead brothers raised from the deep swamp;
the psychedelic band that says no to drugs, StarZombieCruzer;
Zombie!Fuck? killing whitey, bringing him back to life and killing him again;
the sole surviving RiffZombieRat, Mr Kitten-Tank;
The Undead Surfer - Thee Mysterious Tapeman Zombie, with his reanimated tape orchestra;

and shake your bones and rattle your eyeballs out with The Lovehater Zombies.

Crowning of the best dressed zombie king and queen of the prom with prizes galore. Starts 9pm sharp. Don't forget your corsage.
