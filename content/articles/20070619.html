Zombie Prom II
Dance of the dead

Happy. Saturday 7/07/07, 8:30pm. $12, or $10 with costume

Some things won't stay down... even after they die they wanna dance

Postmoderncore presents the second annual Zombie Prom at Happy (cnr Tory and Vivian St.) On Saturday July 7th exhume yourself in your glad-rags for a fleshed out sequel. Free Zombie compilation for the first 50 corpses to stumble through the door. Featuring music by Knife Fight, The Smokers, Volcana, Murderbike, The Riff Rats, Dungeon Master and The Windups.

Monstrous music all night with (un)live acts:

The Windups shaking bones in a carnival of souls
Guttural vermin from the pet cemetery The Riff Rats
Volcana boy eats girl two pieces
Terror creatures from the grave Knife Fight play ugly
Dungeon Master will be bringing the doom of inferno dei morti-viventi
From the swamp fire hell of the living dead come The Smokers
And
Riding into town for the first time chopper chicks from zombie town (Dunedin) Murderbike

Crowning of the best dressed zombie king and queen of the prom with prizes galore gore. Starts 8:30 sharp. Don't forget your corsage.

Those who rose from the grave for the first Zombie Prom in 2006 can be seen at
