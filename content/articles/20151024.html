Postmoderncore is really excited to welcome a new artist to the roster, Death Masks. Their first album for Postmoderncore is Black as a Color. "The world this piece intends to create is shrouded in spectral lights and abysmal darkness, it is a world caught between bestial cruelty and yearning sentimentality.".
